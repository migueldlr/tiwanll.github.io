About

Dice is a tool for looking at multiple rolls of multiple dice. The user inputs the number of dice and the number of times to roll, and a table with the rolls as well as a chart tracking frequencies of the sums of rolls is generated. Let it be noted that values for dice or rolls that are too high may cause the page to freeze or mess up formatting. Dice was written with a heap of PHP, a dash of HTML, and a sprinkle of CSS.
